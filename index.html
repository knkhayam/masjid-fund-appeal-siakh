About This Project

We are undertaking a noble mission to build a masjid at Government Pilot Secondary School SIAKH. This masjid will serve as a spiritual center for students, staff, and the local community, providing a place for daily prayers, Islamic education, and community gatherings.

Your contribution to this project is considered sadaqah jariyah (ongoing charity), which means the rewards will continue to benefit you even after your passing. Every prayer offered in this masjid, every Quran recited, and every Islamic lesson taught will bring you blessings from Allah (SWT).

We have carefully planned all the necessary materials and construction requirements. Your generous donations will help us complete this blessed project and create a lasting legacy for future generations.
